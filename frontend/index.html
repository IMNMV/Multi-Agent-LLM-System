CSV Data Requirements

Fake News Detection

Required Columns:
  • text - Article content to analyze
Strongly Recommended:
  • target or label - Ground truth labels (0 = Fake News, 1 = Real News)
Optional Columns:
  • article_id - Unique identifier
  • source - Article source/publisher
Example CSV:
text,target,article_id,source
"Breaking: New study shows...",1,001,Reuters
"SHOCKING truth revealed...",0,002,Unknown

AI Text Detection

Required Columns:
  • text - Text content to analyze for AI generation
Strongly Recommended:
  • target or label - Ground truth labels (0 = AI-generated, 1 = Human-written)
Optional Columns:
  • article_id - Unique identifier
  • source - Text source/origin
Example CSV:
text,target,article_id
"This essay explores the intersection...",1,001
"The analysis demonstrates that...",0,002

Important Notes

  • Ground truth labels are highly recommended - Without them, visualizations and performance metrics cannot be generated
  • The system auto-detects column names and suggests mappings
  • Alternative column names like content, article, ground_truth are supported
  • CSV files should be UTF-8 encoded
  • Text should be clean (no excessive special characters or formatting)
